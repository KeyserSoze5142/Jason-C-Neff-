An aggressive criminal defense lawyer providing the strongest defense possible.

NEVER speak to the police without consulting us first!

We defend the rights and freedom of the people accused or charged with misdemeanor and felony crimes in both the state and federal systems. When it comes to criminal defense — an aggressive approach is often the best strategy.

We work proactively to evaluate every factual and legal aspect of your case. We work hard to build the strongest possible defense targeted at the weaknesses in the case against you.

Whenever possible, we work toward dismissal or a reduction of the charges against you. We also work to protect your future interests by keeping your case eligible for future expungement when possible.

We work closely with each client, and make sure that each client is fully informed of the progress of their case. When needed, we will use the services of skilled investigators and other professionals who can uncover facts, challenge opposing evidence and support your defense effort.
