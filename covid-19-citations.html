Public Health Violation Citations Lawyer — Tuscaloosa, AL

Protecting students and residents from citations that can impact their future.

During the COVID-19 pandemic, the City of Tuscaloosa took some extremely aggressive approaches to public health violations. The City of Tuscaloosa placed a lot of focus on college students (on and off campus). Although there is nothing in the law that requires you to have an attorney for an ordinance violation, it can be helpful to navigate the Municipal Court here in Tuscaloosa. We have seen an increased number of citations for:

  • Violation of the City Mask Ordinance
  • House Party
  • Social Gathering violations
  • Interference with Police operations

In many of our recent cases, not only are there the aforementioned citations, but also citations for:

  • Minor in Possession of Alcohol
  • Improper ID
  • Resisting Arrest
  • Disorderly Conduct
  • Open Container
  • Public Intoxication

The #1 focus of Neff Law is to ensure that our clients’ futures are not impacted by events such as these.

It is important to note that each of these violations can have fines and court costs that can range from as low as $361.00 to well over $1,000.00, and other court requirements as well. In addition to criminal violations, we can assist with University of Alabama student conduct violations or SNAMs. The University likewise takes a hard-line approach to blatant violations and will not only evict students from dorms but will suspend or expel a student that receives multiple violations.
